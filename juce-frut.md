## Ninja Builds

#### Convert Projucer To Ninja

```console
./FRUT/prefix/FRUT/bin/Jucer2Reprojucer SimpleConvert.jucer ../FRUT/prefix/FRUT/cmake/Reprojucer.cmake --juce-modules=../../../../JUCE/modules
```
